# Review Rubric

Use this rubric to keep the review balanced:

- Contribution: Is the main claim clear and meaningful?
- Correctness: Does the method follow from the assumptions?
- Evidence: Do experiments support the claim?
- Novelty: Is the relationship to prior work clear?
- Reproducibility: Are details sufficient for independent verification?
- Clarity: Can readers understand the method and result?
- Limitations: Are failure modes and boundaries acknowledged?
