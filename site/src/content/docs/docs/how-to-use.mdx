## Choose by Task

Start with the task, not the prompt name. Use the home page recommender or pick one of these paths:

- Reviewing a submitted paper: Full Paper Review Workflow.
- Preparing your own manuscript: Pre-submission Self Review Workflow.
- Responding to reviews: Rebuttal Simulation Workflow.
- Summarizing reviews: Meta-review Synthesis Workflow.

## Provide Evidence

AI-assisted review works best when you provide the paper sections that support the question. Include the abstract, method, experiments, related work, limitations, and venue rubric when available.

## Treat Outputs as Drafts

The output is decision support. Human reviewers and authors remain responsible for judgment, fairness, confidentiality, and final wording.
