title: Safety and Confidentiality
description: Guardrails for AI-assisted peer review.
---

## Core Rules

- Do not upload confidential manuscripts to tools that are not approved for the review context.
- Do not fabricate citations, experimental results, reviewer requirements, or venue policy.
- Separate evidence-backed criticism from speculation.
- Treat AI output as a draft requiring human judgment.
- Preserve reviewer anonymity and author confidentiality.

## Safety Levels

- `L0`: Prompt only.
- `L1`: Prompt plus templates, no scripts.
- `L2`: Prompt plus references and structured assets.
- `L3`: Script-enabled skill that needs extra review before use.
